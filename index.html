Why test automation?

How does it help game development?

Increase development iteration speed

Bugs are found closer to the moment where they're introduced, which makes them quicker to fix, and features easier to evaluate and iterate on.

Free manual QA from monotonous checklists

Convert error-prone daily smokes into automation, so QA can focus on what they do best: exploratory testing, qualatitive feedback, and tracking down repro steps.

Stability throughout development

Mitigate risk through playtesting earlier, and making deep changes late into development, with the support of automation to ensure the game is always playable

Easier than you think!

Automation doesn't require upending processes and making foundational code changes. The right strategic tests can provide value, even late in development.
